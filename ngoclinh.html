DOJA-CAT "CYBER-SEX"

(What a time to be alive)

ABOUT

“Cyber Sex” is the fourth single off American rapper Doja Cat’s second studio album Hot Pink.

The NSFW music video for the track was released along with the album. In it, she becomes a top-rated porn star who then becomes an engineer who creates her ideal boo.

The song first gained traction online after going viral on Twitter and becoming a TikTok trend in January 2020 with multiple trends using the song becoming popular on the app.
